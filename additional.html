Admission Requirements and Procedures

Our programs welcome students from a variety of backgrounds. Applicants must have a high school diploma or equivalent. For graduate programs, a relevant undergraduate degree is required. The application process includes submitting transcripts, a personal statement, and letters of recommendation. Deadlines vary by program, so please check our admissions page for specifics.

Tuition and Fees

Undergraduate tuition is averagely 5,000 cedis per academic year. Graduate program fees vary, starting from 7,000 cedis per year. Additional expenses include lab fees, textbooks, and access to online resources. Financial aid, scholarships, and flexible payment plans are available for eligible students.

Policies and Regulations

Our university upholds strict academic integrity policies. Grading is based on a standard A-F scale. Attendance, participation, and adherence to course requirements are mandatory. Students are encouraged to familiarize themselves with all academic policies and student conduct guidelines.
